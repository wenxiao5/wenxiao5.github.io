About Me

I use economic theory to study business problems. My research interests include game theory, behavioral economics, and quantitative marketing.

I am currently a PhD student at the Quantitative Marketing Group at UC Berkeley. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, and Economics from University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in May 2023 and was fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Song Lin. Prior to beginning at Illinois, I studied at Zhejiang University for two years.
