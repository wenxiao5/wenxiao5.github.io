About Me

I am a PhD student at the Quantitative Marketing Group at UC Berkeley. My research interests include game theory, behavioral economics, and quantitative marketing.

I obtained my bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, and Computational Science & Engineering from University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in May 2023 and was fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Song Lin. Prior to beginning at Illinois, I studied at Zhejiang University for two years.
